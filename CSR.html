RamanaSoft Foundation

RamanaSoft Foundation is the CSR arm of RamanaSoft Corp Ltd. With its core efforts tilted towards education and health – the central pillars of a socio-economically strong nation – RamanaSoft Foundation is building accelerators to attain a better, brighter future for the children of tomorrow.

SOCIAL ENHANCEMENT PROGRAM

RamanaSoft Foundation is working towards holistically improving the quality of education for the weaker sections of the society. We do this by focusing not only on the physical environment where learning happens but also on the way stakeholders of the school interact with each other. We adopt and transform schools with the awareness that differences reside even in a single-mile radius. Every community, locale, and school, is unique in its organization, administration, and function. Since 2014, we adopted 75 government schools across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu, and has benefited 65000+ children in adopted schools.

Student Enrichment

Enabling learning through resource upgradation, interactive course content, and skill-based learning programs

School Upgradation

Infusing a joyful learning experience in 75 schools by offering basic amenities on need-basis

Health & Well-being

Enhancing a child’s physical and mental health by improving school infrastructure, and water & sanitation facilities

Teacher Mentoring

Helping teachers grow professionally by developing skill-enhancement programs and offering mental & physical health support
